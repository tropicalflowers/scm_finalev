INSTRUCTIONS FOR THE GAME

1. Shuffle and lay out all cards face down.
2. Flip two cards at a time to find matching pairs.
3. If cards match, keep them face up.
4. If cards don't match, flip them back down.
5. Continue until all pairs are found.
6. Record the total time taken to complete the game.
